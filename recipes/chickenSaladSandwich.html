Chicken Salad Sandwich Recipe

Very delicious sandwich that my mum use to make for me as a child

Divine!

Ingredients

  • Any Chicken [if you get the drumsticks or any chicken with bones you can create the chicken noodle soup recipe
  • Celery
  • Hard-Boiled Eggs
  • Onions
  • Mayonaise
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Bread

Steps

  1. Boil the chicken and strip it of its meat after it is cooked
  2. Just cut everything and mix everything together
  3. Put in bread
