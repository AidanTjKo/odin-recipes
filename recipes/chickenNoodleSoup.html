Chicken Noodle Soup Recipe

Kill Two Birds with One Stone. You can use the bones from the Chicken Salad Sandwich

Candy Crush!

Ingredients

  • Chicken broth w/t bones
  • Carrots
  • Bok Choy
  • Noodles
  • Salt

Steps

  1. Keep chicken broth and bones from Chicken Salad Sandwich add some salt to season
  2. Cut up vegetables and toss them in the broth to blanch
  3. Once they are soft you can put in the noodles
  4. Once the noodles are cooked you can eat it straight from the pot
