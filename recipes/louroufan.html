Lou Rou Fan Recipe

This is a classic Taiwanese dish consisting of minced pork and marinated hard-boiled egg served over some rice.

Delicious!

Ingredients

  • Minced Pork
  • Bok Choy
  • Hard-Boiled Eggs
  • Five-spice powder
  • Shallots
  • Sugar
  • Soy Sauce
  • Water

Steps

  1. Cook the minced pork until brown
  2. Add the soy sauce + sugar + shallots + five spice and stir
  3. Add the hard boiled eggs in and fill the pot up until it covers everything
  4. Let sit for around 45 min [the best eggs are best if left marinated overnight]
